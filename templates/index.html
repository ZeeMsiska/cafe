    About GreenHouse

    Established in 2023, Greenhouse has been a labor of love from the very beginning. It all started with a shared passion for exceptional coffee and a belief that the perfect cup could bring joy to anyone's day.

    Our founder, Rehema M'bang'ombe embarked on a quest to source the finest coffee beans from around the world and create an oasis for coffee enthusiasts.

    • Culinary Excellence: Striving for exceptional food quality using locally sourced, fresh ingredients and expert culinary techniques, ensuring a delightful taste in every dish..
    • Ambiance and Experience: Creating a welcoming, serene, and intimate atmosphere where guests can relax, unwind, and enjoy a memorable dining experience, especially during night dates, with attention to lighting, decor, and overall ambiance..
    • Customer Satisfaction: Ensuring exceptional service and attentiveness to customers' needs, whether it's providing a tranquil space for a morning coffee or curating a romantic evening setting for couples.
